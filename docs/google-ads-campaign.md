## Optimisation Schedule

### Week 1–2 (Learning Phase)
- Launch all campaigns paused, enable one per day
- Monitor search terms report daily — add negatives
- Check conversion tracking is firing (GA4 Realtime)

### Week 3–4
- Review search term reports — mine for new keywords, add negatives
- Pause low-performing keywords (high spend, no conversions)
- Test different headline pin positions in RSAs
- Assess which ad groups drive StoreKit vs Deliveroo vs Uber Eats clicks

### Month 2+
- Shift budget toward highest-ROAS campaigns
- Graduate from Maximize Clicks to Target CPA on brand campaign
- Build remarketing audiences from GA4 (visited menu but didn't click order)
- A/B test landing pages (direct to StoreKit vs homepage)
- Adjust location radius based on conversion data
- Add seasonal ad copy (summer salads, winter soups)

### Ongoing Weekly
- Review search terms — add negatives
- Check CPA by campaign and ad group
- Monitor click-through rates — refresh underperforming ad copy
- Compare StoreKit vs delivery platform conversion rates
- Adjust bids for time-of-day performance
